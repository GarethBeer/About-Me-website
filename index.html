About me

I have a passion for IT and have from an early age but have never done anything with it, I have always been fairly technically minded and enjoyed tinkering on computers as well as taking them apart just for the sake of it. That is why I decided to take the plunge and book on the MCR codes software engineering fastrack course because it would give me the push to turn a geniune interest and passion into a career. A career that is currently in high demand. Having spent evenings completing challenges on codeacademy and freecodecamp I thought it was time to put my money where my mouth is and prove to myself and others that I can do this for real. My dream would be to find work within the snow sports industry or the adventure tourism industry as a developer so that I could combine my two passions into a career which is based around what I love doing. Wish me luck!

Skiing

Skiing is another love of mine! I spent two years living in the canadian rockies and over the course of two winters I spent well 250 days on snow. I then moved to the ski hills in Aus and spent over another 100 days on snow. That was a combination of instructing and free skiing. I thoroughly enjoyed the instructing side of things but free skiing is where it is at. The benefit of doing seasons is you aren't a slave to the weather like you are on holidays. I haven't been since I got home from Aus last October so I am desperate to go out this season for a week or two.
